VERSION: 1.13d

CODE:

000D528F   83BD 8C000000 08 CMP DWORD PTR SS:[EBP+8C],8
000E5D4D   83BD 8C000000 08 CMP DWORD PTR SS:[EBP+8C],8

WHAT TO DO:
Change the second operands to 4.

HOW TO FIND THE OFFSET:

When you search for the command: CMP DWORD PTR SS:[EBP+8C],8 
there will only be two of them in D2Game.dll.

1.13d Offsets: D528F, E5D4D
1.13c Offsets: 4A9CD, CADDF
1.12 Offsets: 6424F, EBD3D
