Vanilla Frosting 4.6.4 for 1.13d 
By Jonathan Vasquez (FearedBliss)
Released on Thursday, September 12, 2013
-----------------------------------------------

Installation:
-------------
1. Make sure to backup your current Diablo II directory before installing. 
   A simple copy/paste of your current Diablo II folder will do.

2. Extract and replace the following files into your Diablo II directory:

-> Patch_D2.mpq
-> D2Game.dll
-> D2gfx.dll

 * Only copy the D2Client.dll if you had a previous version of Vanilla Frosting.
  > The D2Client.dll included in this release is just a stock D2Client.dll.

3. Enjoy.

Uninstallation:
Delete your Diablo II folder, and rename your old backup copy of Diablo II 
to 'Diablo II'.
